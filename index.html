01 / About

Who I am

I make Roblox games, mostly working on the side players don't think about — saving data right, handling money/currency without it breaking, catching cheaters before they ruin a server, that kind of thing. I'm picky about my code: small targeted fixes over big rewrites, nothing fancy for the sake of it, just stuff that holds up when a few thousand people are playing at once.

02 / Skills

What I work with

Economy Systems

Data saving with ProfileService, passive income, offline earnings, prestige, upgrade trees.

Anti-Cheat

Catching exploiters in real time — heartbeat checks, character monitoring, instant alerts.

Networking

MemoryStoreService, RemoteEvents, keeping the server in charge so clients can't fake things.

Gameplay Logic

Server-driven mechanics, ownership/possession tracking, hitbox detection, physics tools.

Tooling

Studio-MCP workflows and small custom tools that make iterating on a game faster.

Data Integrity

Attribute-based state, quests, rewards, streaks — stuff that has to survive a bad connection.
