Who I am

I'm a Roblox developer focused on the backend — the systems that handle data, enforce fairness, and keep games running smoothly at scale. My work spans ProfileService-driven economies, server-authoritative gameplay logic, anti-cheat detection, and networking layers like MemoryStoreService.
